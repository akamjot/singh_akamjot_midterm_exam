INDOOR SPORTS VS OUTDOOR SPORTS

Outdoor Sports

Football

Reducing body fat. Building strength, stamina and speed.
Training your brain, improving concentration and coordination. Promoting teamwork, being social and boosting your confidence.

Cycling

Cycling can help to protect you from serious diseases such as stroke, heart attack, some cancers, depression, diabetes, obesity and arthritis.
Riding a bike is healthy, fun and a low-impact form of exercise for all ages.
Cycling is easy to fit into your daily routine by riding to the shops, park, school or work.

Mountain Climbing

As a compound exercise that utilises multiple muscle groups in your whole body, mountain climbers are an effective way of strengthening your arms, back, shoulders, core and legs. Another benefit of using multiple muscles at once is an increased heart rate, which will help you burn more calories.

Overll, playing outdoor games benefits the individual im multifarious ways as it keeps a person away from heart diseases and build stamina and also increases the physical abilities.
